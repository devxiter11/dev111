Welcome to Nepal

Explore the beauty, culture, and heritage of Nepal!

Nepal is a landlocked country located in South Asia, nestled mainly in the Himalayas between China and India. It is known for its majestic mountain ranges, including Mount Everest, the highest peak in the world. The country's diverse geography ranges from the flat Terai plains to rugged hills and soaring snowy peaks.

Nepal has a rich cultural heritage, home to multiple ethnic groups and religions that coexist peacefully. It is the birthplace of Lord Buddha, and Lumbini remains a major pilgrimage site for Buddhists around the world. The country's festivals, music, and traditional dances reflect its vibrant cultural mosaic.

Tourism plays a vital role in Nepal's economy. Many visitors come for trekking, mountaineering, and spiritual retreats. Popular destinations include Kathmandu Valley, Pokhara, Chitwan National Park, and the Annapurna Circuit. With its natural beauty and warm hospitality, Nepal leaves a lasting impression on those who visit.
